About Me

I am a data scientist and behavioural economist, developing technology that helps consumers make better decisions. Most of my applied work is in banking and retail finance. In collaboration with my colleagues and other experts, I've been lucky enough to work on policies and digital tools that save people time, money, and hassle. My current focus is using AI and personalized data representations to drive smarter financial decisions.
